# punk_os

PunkOS is an application written in Flutter, running on Android.

PunkOS is not a normal application, it much likes a Operate System. Because I plan to develop many applications inside PunkOS.

PunkOS is a open platform, by using low-code tech, every one can develop her/his app themselves. 

# Why?

The digital world today made me sad.

Both Android and iOS gives user too few freedom.

Not only phones, but so are people. People today are too busy. The little phone is her/his best friend. I'm very sad for this.

People spend all days on phone. But phone are full of business to made people stupid and mad.

Look at techs today, though your phone has 256GB/512GB storage, all your important data are stored at **Cloud**. 

There is no cloud, but other's computer. The bad is you even don't own that computer. You rent it.

Your life, your most valuable things are not own by yourself. Your life is rent by a yearly plan. If you stop paying it, you lost your life.

What was worse, the mobile operate systems kills your creativity. 

Normal people cannot customize the phone to behaviors what they want. Only the apps customizes the users to what the companies want.

So I decide to develop PunkOS. I want using it to woke the creativity.
